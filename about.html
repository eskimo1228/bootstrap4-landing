About ReVet

ReVet was founded with the core belief that good employees deserve better opportunities…and great companies deserve the best employees.

ReVet believes in the power of references. We’re committed to helping people who have demonstrated a good work ethic, talent, and integrity. These people invariably have others who will speak to their abilities…current or former supervisors, clients, colleagues, friends, or teachers. ReVet makes it easy for those who have demonstrated value to connect their references with great employers and others.

ReVet’s founders and team are company owners, leaders, mentors, and employees themselves. For over 20 years they have hired and developed thousands of employees in several industries, from Fitness to Service & Hospitality to Technology.
